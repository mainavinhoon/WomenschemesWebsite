  • Vision:

    • Our vision is to facilitate women to become economically and socially empowered , so that they contribute healthy and equal partners in an environment free from violence and discrimination. Provision of opportunities to children and adolescent girls for growth and development to the fullest in safe and protected environment.

    Women have been part of social torture since ancient times and are always ranked and preferred last in terms of work load and capabilities which makes them feel deprived and depressed. Our motto is to make them feel equal and make them feel they are no less than the other sections of the society and provide them the right information related to their benefits . We as a team focus on authentic information and no bluffing so that women really in need can fully utilize our website.
    In future we expect to widen and add more information to our website. Our website would observe more updates in future too.
