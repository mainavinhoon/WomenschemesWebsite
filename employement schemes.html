  • Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana

    • → Features of the Scheme:

    → The Gujarat government has initiated a scheme known as Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana lending interest-free loans to women self-help group in Gujarat

    → Under this scheme, 50, 000 JLEGs will be formed in urban parts of the Gujarat
    → Through the Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana, 50,000 Joint Liability Earnings & Savings Groups will be formed in rural regions of Gujarat.
    → Around 10 lakh women will be receiving the benefits under MMUY Gujarat.
    → Each self-help group must include ten members under this scheme.


    • How to Apply Online under Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana?


    → In order to apply online under MMUY, women self-help groups must visit the official website of the scheme and should fill the Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana Online Registration/ Application
    → You will get to witness the homepage of the women empowerment-based scheme by the Gujarat Government.


    • Eligibility Criteria:


    → The applicant needs to be a permanent native of Gujarat.
    → The applicant should be women under this Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh.
    → The applicant needs to be an integral part of the self-help group of Gujarat State.
    → The self-help group should have ten members.
    → The government is engrossed in providing loans to these groups & interest will be paid by the concerned state government in the banks.


    • Benefits of Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana:


    → Through this scheme, women from Gujarat are attaining interest-free loans.
    → The scheme is considered as a new life donation to the women of Gujarat state.
    → The Government of Gujarat established a scheme to bestow self-employment to women with a mission to accelerate the pace of self-employment and to elevate the empowerment of women in the Gujarat state.
    → Those who are permanent residents of the Gujarat state will be provided with the benefits of the scheme.
    → Women can start any of their jobs according to their knowledge and expertise.
    → Women will be attaining respect in a society ruled by men due to their own employment via the Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana.
